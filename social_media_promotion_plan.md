# Social Media Promotion Plan for Rustic Roots Artisan Sourdough Subscription

## Objective
To promote the Rustic Roots artisan sourdough subscription landing page across various social media platforms to increase traffic and subscriptions.

## Target Audience
- Food enthusiasts
- Health-conscious consumers
- Artisan bread lovers
- Local community members

## Key Messages
- Experience the joy of artisan sourdough delivered to your door.
- Join a community of sourdough lovers and elevate your baking skills.
- Fresh, organic ingredients sourced locally.

## Social Media Platforms
1. **Instagram**  
   - **Content Types:** High-quality images of sourdough, behind-the-scenes baking videos, customer testimonials, and user-generated content.  
   - **Frequency:** 3-4 posts per week, daily stories.  
   - **Hashtags:** #RusticRoots #SourdoughSubscription #ArtisanBread #FreshlyBaked  
   - **Engagement:** Host a giveaway for new subscribers.  

2. **Facebook**  
   - **Content Types:** Blog posts about the benefits of sourdough, customer stories, and promotional offers.  
   - **Frequency:** 2-3 posts per week.  
   - **Engagement:** Create a Facebook group for subscribers to share recipes and tips.  

3. **Twitter**  
   - **Content Types:** Quick tips on sourdough baking, links to the landing page, and engagement with food influencers.  
   - **Frequency:** 5-7 tweets per week.  
   - **Engagement:** Participate in relevant Twitter chats.  

4. **Pinterest**  
   - **Content Types:** Pin images of sourdough recipes, baking tips, and subscription details.  
   - **Frequency:** 5-10 pins per week.  
   - **Engagement:** Collaborate with food bloggers for shared boards.  

## Paid Advertising
- **Instagram and Facebook Ads:** Targeted ads showcasing the subscription service with a call-to-action to visit the landing page.
- **Budget:** Allocate $500 for the first month to test ad performance.

## Metrics for Success
- Website traffic from social media platforms.
- Number of new subscriptions.
- Engagement rates (likes, shares, comments).
- Growth of social media followers.

## Timeline
- **Week 1:** Launch initial posts and ads.
- **Week 2:** Analyze engagement and adjust strategy as needed.
- **Week 3-4:** Continue promotion and gather feedback from subscribers.

## Conclusion
This social media promotion plan aims to leverage various platforms to effectively reach and engage our target audience, driving traffic to the Rustic Roots artisan sourdough subscription landing page.
